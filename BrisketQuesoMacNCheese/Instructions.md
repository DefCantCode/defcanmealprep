[Brisket Queso Mac N' Cheese](https://www.facebook.com/reel/951445297821974)
*560 calories - 46G Protein*
- 1 flat cut brisket
    - trim exterior fat
    - slice into small chunks
- throw in Slow Cooker

## Flavor
- 900G (32 OZ) Flat Cut Brisket
- 400G Fire-Roasted diced Tomato Sauce
- 120G Red Enchilada Sauce
- 45G Green Chiles
- 60G Honey
- 1TBSP Salt
- 2 TBSP Onion Powder
- 2 TSP Black Pepper

# Pasta Sauce
- Blended Queso Sauce
- 800G 2% Cottage Cheese
- 100G American Cheese
- 150G Extra Sharp Cheddar Cheese
- 80G Red Enchilada Sauce
- 5G Nutritional Yeast
- Sprinkle Salt and Pepper
- 120-240ML Milk *until pourable*

# Cook
- High: 4 - 5 hours or Low 5 - 6 hours
- Shred once done
- Add 672G Pasta Cooked to 50%
- Mix
- Add pasta sauce and mix
- cook for 20 more minutes
- add cilantro
