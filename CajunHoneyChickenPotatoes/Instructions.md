## Chicken
- 1000g chicken breast cut to cubes (normal tray)
*Chicken Seasonings*
- Salt
- Pepper
- Garlic
- Onion
- Oregano
- Chili Flakes
- Olive Oil
*hand mix chicken*
- 1200g potatoes (size reference 3 large, 8 medium, 17 small)
**Potatoes**
- chop potatoes into cubes
*Potato Seasoning*
- Garlic
- Italian Herbs
- Paprika
- Olive Oil
*hand mix*
**Cooking**
- Air Fry 20 minutes *potatoes*
- Place chicken in Light butter *medium heat 6 minutes*
- Lower heat add slice butter
**Mid Cook Seasoning**
- Garlic
- Onion
- Paprika
- Oregano
- Drizzle Honey
- Chopped parsley
**Potato Sauce**
- Chopped Onion *3-4 minutes*
- Tomato Paste *Two minutes*
- 250ml Light Evaporated Milk
- 150g Garlic & Herb Cream Cheese *low heat stir*
- Add potatoes once smooth *mix*
- Finally separate out
