- 2 Pounds Chicken Breast

- layer of salt
- layer of pepper
- layer of italian Seasoning

*light Mix*

## Cook For 4 Hours
*Crockpot*

- add bag of pepperoni
- 2 cups of cheese
- Mix until melted
